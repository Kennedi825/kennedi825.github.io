Pierce The Veil

Pierce The Veil is a band originating and residing in San Diego, California. The band consists of one bassist, two guitarists, and one drummer. The four members are Vic, Mike, Jaime, and Tony. Vic and Mike are brothers. Tony and Jaime were in another band together before joining the Fuentes brothers to form Pierce The Veil.

Pierce The Veil are part of the 2015 Warped Tour line-up. They join many other bands to travel the country and preform. They announced their return to the tour after the end of their World Tour. Sleeping with Sirens joined them on their World Tour, but unlike Pierce The Veil, Sleeping with Sirens will not be at Warped. They started their "We like it quiet" Tour directly after the World Tour had ended.

Vic Fuentes.

Victor Vincent (Vic) Fuentes

Vic Fuentes is 32 years old (born February 10,1983). He plays guitar and sings in Pierce The Veil. He has also played the keyboard on some of the bands albums. He was in a band called "Before Today" before he and his brother, Mike Fuentes, formed Pierce The Veil. He also writes most of the bands songs. Vic is dating a woman named Danielle Victoria.

Tony Perry.

Cesar Antonio Perry Soto (Tony Perry)

Tony Perry is 29 years old (Born on February 25, 1986 in Tijuana, Mexico). He also plays guitar in Pierce The Veil. He learned to play the guitar from his grandfather when he was 15. He joined Pierce The Veil in 2007. Tony is dating a woman named Erin Aschow.

Jaime Preciado.

Jaime (pronounced "Hi-me") Alberto Preciado

Jaime Preciado is 29 years old (born on May 17, 1986). Jaime plays bass in Pierce The Veil. He joined the band in 2007. Before joining Pierce The Veil Jaime played bass in a metalcore band called "Trigger My Nightmare". In 2014, Jaime won Best Bassist at the first Alternative Press Awards. Jaime is dating a woman named Jessica Rose.

Mike Fuentes.

Michael Christopher (Mike) Fuentes

Mike Fuentes is 31 years old(born December 14, 1984). Mike plays drums for Pierce The Veil. He has also played for the metal group "Isles & Glaciers". He has also started a solo rap project under the stage name "MikeWhiskeyHands" where he raps on instrumentals produced by Jaime Preciado. Mike is Vic Fuentes' brother. Mike is dating a woman named Alysha Nett.

Pierce The Veils albums include: A Flair for the Dramatic, Selfish Machines, Collide with the Sky, and their new single The Divine Zero, which was released on June 18, 2015.
